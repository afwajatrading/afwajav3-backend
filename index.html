Rental Terms & Conditions

Required Documents

Valid driving license, original IC / Passport, and latest utility bill (water/electric) or student card.

Security Deposit

A refundable security deposit is required upon vehicle handover. Will be refunded within 3-7 working days after return.

Fuel Policy

Vehicles must be returned with the same fuel level as when collected. Surcharges apply for insufficient fuel.

Driving Area Limits

Vehicles are strictly for use within Peninsular Malaysia. Off-road driving or track racing is strictly prohibited.

Traffic Fines & Summons

The renter is fully responsible for all traffic summons, parking tickets, and tolls incurred during the rental period.

Extension & Cancellation

Extensions must be informed 24 hours prior. Cancellations made less than 48 hours will forfeit the booking fee.
